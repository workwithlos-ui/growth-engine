## Idea 1: "Obsidian Precision"

**Design Movement**: Swiss International / Neo-Brutalist Minimalism (Linear, Stripe, Vercel aesthetic)

**Core Principles**:
1. Radical reduction. Every element earns its place or gets cut.
2. Typography as architecture. The type IS the design.
3. Negative space as a luxury signal. Emptiness = confidence.
4. Monochrome dominance with surgical accent color.

**Color Philosophy**:
- Background: #0a0a0a (near-black, warmer than pure black)
- Surface cards: #111111 with 1px border of rgba(255,255,255,0.06)
- Primary text: #f5f5f5 (soft white, not harsh)
- Secondary text: #737373 (muted gray)
- Accent: #22c55e (a single green for CTAs, active states, data highlights)
- No gradients. No color splashes. Just black, white, gray, and one green.

**Layout Paradigm**:
- Full-width sections with max-w-5xl content
- Asymmetric hero with left-aligned headline
- Stacked vertical rhythm with generous 120-160px section gaps
- Cards in 2-3 column grids with equal gutters
- No sidebar. No complex grids. Just vertical flow.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thin horizontal rule dividers between sections (rgba white 6%)
2. Numbered steps with oversized monospace numbers (01, 02, 03, 04)
3. Subtle dot-grid or fine-line background texture on hero

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Hover states: cards lift with subtle translateY and border brightens
- Buttons: solid fill with no border-radius (sharp corners = precision)
- Links: underline on hover, no color change
- Everything feels intentional and mechanical

**Animation**:
- Fade-up on scroll (staggered, 100ms delay between siblings)
- No bouncing, no spring physics. Linear easing only.
- Hero text types in or fades up word by word
- Cards appear with opacity 0 to 1 + translateY 20px to 0

**Typography System**:
- Headlines: Inter Tight or Geist, 700 weight, tight letter-spacing (-0.02em)
- Body: Inter or Geist, 400 weight, 16-18px, relaxed line-height
- Labels/badges: 11-12px uppercase, 600 weight, wide letter-spacing (0.08em)
- Monospace numbers: JetBrains Mono or SF Mono for step numbers and data

## Idea 2: "Void Architecture"

**Design Movement**: Deconstructivist Digital / Anti-Design (inspired by Nothing Phone, Teenage Engineering)

**Core Principles**:
1. Exposed structure. Show the grid, show the system.
2. Tension through asymmetry. Nothing is perfectly centered.
3. Raw materials. Monospace type, wireframe-like borders, exposed mechanics.
4. Information density without clutter through careful layering.

**Color Philosophy**:
- Background: #0a0a0a
- Cards: transparent with dashed borders (#333)
- Text: #e5e5e5 primary, #666 secondary
- Accent: #ff6b35 (burnt orange, industrial, unexpected)
- Highlight: dotted underlines instead of color fills

**Layout Paradigm**:
- Broken grid with intentional misalignment
- Hero text pushed to extreme left with massive font size
- Sections overlap slightly with negative margins
- Information presented in terminal-like blocks
- Footer as a dense information strip

**Signature Elements**:
1. Dashed borders and exposed grid lines
2. Corner markers (crosshair-like registration marks)
3. Monospace metadata labels floating near content

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Hover: elements shift position slightly (1-2px)
- Cursor changes to crosshair on interactive areas
- Click feedback: brief invert flash
- Scroll: parallax depth on layered elements

**Animation**:
- Glitch-style text reveals on hero
- Elements slide in from unexpected directions
- Staggered grid items with random delay offsets
- Subtle scan-line effect on section transitions

**Typography System**:
- Headlines: Space Grotesk, 800 weight, massive sizes (72-96px)
- Body: IBM Plex Mono, 400 weight, 14px
- Labels: Uppercase monospace, 10px, letter-spacing 0.15em
- Numbers: Tabular figures, oversized, semi-transparent

## Idea 3: "Carbon Fiber"

**Design Movement**: Automotive Luxury Digital (Porsche Design, McLaren, Whoop aesthetic)

**Core Principles**:
1. Performance aesthetic. Every element suggests speed and precision.
2. Material honesty. Surfaces feel like real materials (glass, carbon, metal).
3. Data as beauty. Numbers and metrics are the decoration.
4. Restrained luxury. Expensive feel without ostentation.

**Color Philosophy**:
- Background: #0a0a0a with subtle radial gradient to #0f0f0f
- Cards: rgba(255,255,255,0.03) with glass-morphism blur
- Primary text: #ffffff
- Secondary text: #888888
- Accent: #00d4ff (electric cyan, performance, technology)
- Subtle warm highlight: #f59e0b for badges/tags

**Layout Paradigm**:
- Wide cinematic sections (max-w-6xl)
- Hero with centered headline, massive, with a subtle glow behind text
- Bento grid for features (mixed card sizes)
- Horizontal scroll for use cases on mobile
- Floating nav with glass blur effect

**Signature Elements**:
1. Glassmorphism cards with backdrop-blur and subtle border glow
2. Radial gradient spotlights behind key sections
3. Metric counters with animated number transitions

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Cards: glass border brightens on hover, subtle inner glow
- Buttons: gradient fill with shimmer effect on hover
- Smooth magnetic cursor effect near CTAs
- Scroll-triggered counter animations for metrics

**Animation**:
- Smooth fade-up with slight scale (0.98 to 1)
- Gradient background shifts slowly (ambient movement)
- Number counters animate from 0 to value on scroll
- Subtle particle or noise texture animation in hero

**Typography System**:
- Headlines: Satoshi or Plus Jakarta Sans, 700 weight, clean geometric
- Body: Inter, 400 weight, 16px, generous line-height (1.7)
- Labels: 11px uppercase, 500 weight, wide tracking
- Metrics: Tabular lining figures, large and bold
